PRAISE

STUDENT FRIENDLY

Praise is a youthful gospel choir all about the joy of singing and dancing together.


Born from a desire to share the excitement and community of gospel music, Praise is a newly started concept where young people can express themselves through music and movement.

Praise meets every other Monday in even weeks, and everyone - regardless of their beliefs - can join in and have fun.

As of Wednesday, December 4th, Praise officially became a local association under @yfcdk. YFC shares the same values as Praise, which gives a great comfort in being part of the YFC team. You can find the link to become a member on their website.

WHAT THEY OFFER

FREE to Join

There's no cost to participate, and you're welcome to attend whenever you like without any commitment.

This is ideal for students looking to explore new activities without financial commitment.

Open for Everyones

Praise is both fun and inclusive - you don't need to be religious to participate.

Praise is for anyone who wants to step out of their comfort zone and embrace the joy of singing and dancing with enthusiasm.

Accessible

Held every other Monday, it's an easy-to-access weekly event that students can fit into their busy academic life.

Good Vibes on a Monday

Replace your dull Monday with energy and excitement as we gather to sing and dance together in Turbinehallen or Tivoli Friheden
